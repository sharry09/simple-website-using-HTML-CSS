Rachel Green (played by Jennifer Aniston)

Rachel starts as a fashion-conscious woman who works at Central Perk, but she evolves throughout the series. She is known for her romantic entanglements, especially with Ross, and her transition from being financially dependent on her parents to becoming a successful career woman.

Ross Geller (played by David Schwimmer)

Ross is Monica's brother and a paleontologist. He's known for his intelligence and his on-again, off-again relationship with Rachel. He has a tendency to be awkward and neurotic.

Monica Geller (played by Courteney Cox):

Monica is Ross's sister and is obsessive about cleanliness and organization. She is a talented chef and is known for her competitive spirit, especially when it comes to games and cooking.

Chandler Bing (played by Matthew Perry)

Chandler is known for his sarcastic sense of humor and his job in IT. He has a complicated relationship with commitment and is known for his catchphrases and witty one-liners.

Joey Tribbiani (played by Matt LeBlanc)

Joey is a struggling actor who is often portrayed as the least intelligent of the group. He is known for his love of food, particularly pizza, and his catchphrase, "How you doin'?"

Phoebe Buffay (played by Lisa Kudrow)

Phoebe is known for her eccentric and quirky personality. She has a mysterious past and is a talented singer-songwriter. She often shares bizarre and humorous anecdotes from her life.

These six characters form the core ensemble of "Friends" and are at the center of the show's comedic and emotional moments. The dynamics and relationships among these characters are key to the series' enduring popularity.
